## Nozzle Tip Usage Reference

| Tip | Use |
|---|---|
| Black (soap) | Chemical application via built-in injector, for siding. Always paired with a 10–15 min dwell time before rinsing. |
| 40° (white) | Gentle rinse — siding rinse-off after chemical dwell, cars, delicate surfaces. |
| 25° (green) | Standard concrete **spot-work only** — edges/steps/small patio areas the surface cleaner can't reach. **NEVER on siding/walls.** |
| 15° (yellow) | Aggressive concrete-only spot work for stubborn stains. **NEVER on siding/walls** — use cautiously, test first. |

**Siding/wall process:** black tip applies chemical bottom-to-top (even dosing, avoids
streaking) → 10–15 min dwell → 40° tip rinses top-to-bottom (gravity carries dirt away
cleanly). 25°/15° never touch siding — risk of paint strip, stucco crack, or forcing
water behind siding into the wall cavity.

**Pre-wetting landscaping** before chemical work uses a separate garden hose, **not**
the pressure washer at all (even at 40° it's too much pressure/disruption for delicate
plants) — bring a garden hose as gear, connect to the same spigot.
