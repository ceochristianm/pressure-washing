# Offers & Pricing

## The 3-tier menu (never quote a single price)
| Tier | Name | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Standard Compliance Wash | $350 | Low-pressure siding flush (HOA letter fix) |
| 2 | Curb Appeal Package | $650 | Tier 1 + driveway/sidewalk surface cleaning |
| 3 | Total Estate Renewal | $1,250 | Tier 2 + tile roof soft-wash + plant shield |

Presented verbally after satellite quote. Majority of affluent homeowners choose
middle/top when given structured options. Target blended AJS: $550+.

## The test-patch upsell (highest-leverage sales move)
On every siding-only booking: hook up → run surface cleaner on a 3x3 ft driveway patch
for 5 seconds → show homeowner the contrast → offer the full driveway NOW at an
add-on rate (~$200 vs $400 standalone, "rig's already fired up").
Track attempt + close in data/jobs.csv. NOTE: the 80% close rate from the brainstorm
is unverified — measure ours and calibrate.

## Portfolio pricing (jobs 1–3 only)
$200–250 Tier 1 rate, explicitly traded for a Google review + photo rights.
Never free; never below chemicals + fuel + $100.

## Realtor pricing
15% "preferred realtor" discount on rack rates + 24-hr priority booking +
pay-at-closing option on listed properties. Volume relationship > single-job margin.

## Discounting rules
- Neighbor discount (door-hanger, same street, same day): up to 15% — drive time saved is real margin
- Never discount Tier 3 (premium buyers don't need it)
- Every discount gets a reason attached ("already on your street", "portfolio", "realtor partner") — unreasoned discounts train price-shopping
