# Offers & Pricing — À La Carte + Combo Structure

> Restructured 2026-07-18: replaced rigid Tier 1/2/3 with à la carte base services +
> combo discounts. More flexible for real customer requests (many want ONLY
> driveway, or ONLY roof, not the full bundle). Cross-referenced against SOUTH
> FLORIDA-ONLY sources — West Palm Beach-specific data, Palm Beach County
> competitor pricing (Palm Beach Exterior Wash), and Palm Beach/Martin/St. Lucie
> county data (FoxHaven). Central Florida, national, and other-state comps were
> deliberately EXCLUDED — South Florida is a more affluent market than Central FL
> or the national average, and blending in cheaper regional data would have
> under-priced this menu relative to what the actual local market bears.

## HARD FLOOR RULE — never go below this, regardless of relationship or pressure
- **No single service below $200**
- **No multi-service combo below $250**
- If a referral (especially through Dad) asks for a "friend price" below this,
  the answer is no — this floor doesn't cover chemical/gas/time at a real margin.
  When in doubt on any quote: use the Medium price below, or text Christian first
  before going lower.

## Base Services (size-tiered)

### Driveway / Concrete
| Size | Sqft guide | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Small | Under 400 sqft | $200-240 |
| Medium | 400-800 sqft (standard 2-car) | $240-280 |
| Large | 800+ sqft | $280-375 |

### Siding / Walls
| Size | Sqft guide | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Small | Under 1,500 sqft | $350-450 |
| Medium | 1,500-2,500 sqft | $450-650 |
| Large / two-story | 2,500+ sqft OR any 2-story | $650-850 |
(Two-story adds ~25-35% over single-story equivalent — extension wand reach and
extra care genuinely take longer, not just "more square footage.")

### Roof (why it costs more than siding — NOT chemical volume, it's risk + time:
fall/liability exposure, technical difficulty reaching pitched surfaces, longer
job time with the current single-person 12V setup, and a stronger SH mix ~3-4%
vs ~1-1.5% for siding)
| Size | Roof sqft guide | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Small | Under 1,500 sqft roof | $450-550 |
| Medium | 1,500-2,500 sqft roof | $550-750 |
| Large / complex tile | 2,500+ sqft OR steep/complex pitch | $700-950 |

## Bundle Discount Rule (percentage off COMBINED total of selected services)

### 2-Service Bundles: flat 10% off combined total (all sizes)

### Full Package (3 services): scaled by size — FINALIZED 2026-07-18
| Size | Discount | Net profit example (after ~10% variable cost) |
|---|---|---|
| Small | 15% | ~$840 |
| Medium | 20% | ~$1,022 |
| Large | 25% | ~$1,239 |

Rationale: Small full-packages are rare (few homeowners with small homes book
the full bundle) so margin is protected with a smaller discount. Medium is the
realistic middle ground. Large gets the biggest discount specifically because
it's the highest-margin-cushion job and the one worth aggressively selling —
even at 25% off, Large full packages net $1,200+ profit, hitting the explicit
$1K+ profit target.

Negotiation ceiling: respond to price objections with value/differentiation
first (insurance, soft-wash safety), no discount offered yet. If still pushed:
2-bundle max ceiling ~15-18%; full package ceiling is already the finalized
size-scaled % above (do not exceed 25% even on Large). Absolute floor: never
below the sum of individual base prices with zero discount at all. Philosophy:
losing a pure price-shopper to a cheaper competitor is the pricing strategy
working, not failing — that customer was never the target.

## The Two-Touchpoint Upsell Strategy

**Touchpoint 1 — Phone/booking, using satellite view:**
Customer requests one service → get address → pull up Google Maps satellite view
(best default; Zillow aerial/street photos as backup for unclear angles) → quote
the requested service based on visible size → THEN offer: "Since we'll already be
out there, want to add [X] too? If you do both, you get 10% off the combined
total instead of paying full price for each."

**Touchpoint 2 — In-person, on arrival:**
Before starting the booked service, walk the property, note visible issues (roof
staining, siding mold, dirty driveway not in original scope) → offer: "Since I'm
already here, I noticed [specific issue] — if we add that on, you'd get the same
bundle discount as if you'd booked both together. Want me to take care of that
while I'm here?"

Both touchpoints use the same bundle percentage math above — quote the real
discounted total, not a vague "discount."

## Phone/Quoting Mechanics
- **Calling while driving:** pull over first, every time, before looking up
  satellite view — never look up an address while actively driving. Can continue
  the conversation hands-free via earbuds while pulling over.
- **On a job site, machine off:** no special protocol, quote from wherever you're
  standing/sitting.
- **Software:** Google Maps satellite view (free, phone or desktop) is the
  default. Zillow aerial/exterior photos as secondary source if the angle's unclear.
- **Habit:** type the address into Maps the MOMENT they say it, even mid-sentence
  — by the time they finish describing the job, the image is already loaded.

## Realtor / B2B pricing
15% "preferred realtor" discount on the above rack rates + 24-hr priority booking +
pay-at-closing option on listed properties (see b2b-realtors-hoa.md).

## Portfolio pricing (early jobs only, building reviews)
$150-250 flat rate, explicitly traded for a Google review + photo rights. Never
free; never below the hard floor above.
