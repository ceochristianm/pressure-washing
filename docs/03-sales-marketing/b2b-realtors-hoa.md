# B2B: Realtors, HOAs, Property Managers

## Realtors — the priority B2B channel
Ranked by effectiveness (skip networking events — time sink, low-producer density):

### 1. Active MLS listing cold calls (fastest, $0)
Zillow/Redfin → Palm Beach County → 30+ days on market → visible exterior grime in
photos → call the listing agent directly (number on listing).
Script core: reference THEIR listing + THEIR photos specifically → curb appeal =
buyer first impression → can be on-site within 24–48 hrs → price anchor ~$350 →
offer pay-at-closing if helpful.

### 2. Brokerage office walk-ins (highest trust)
High-volume offices (Compass, Coldwell Banker, Illustrated Properties, Keller Williams
in Wellington/Jupiter/PBG). Professional polo + flyers. Ask front desk for managing
broker / preferred-vendor list owner. Leave flyers for top listing agents if unavailable.

### 3. Instagram DM (scalable, VA-delegatable later)
Local hashtags (#WestPalmBeachRealtor etc.) → agents actively posting listings →
DM referencing their specific listing content + 24-hr priority booking + insured +
15% realtor rate + free satellite quote on their next listing.

## The two closing levers
1. **24-hour listing guarantee** — truck on-site within 24 hrs of their call
2. **Pay-at-closing** — invoice on the settlement statement, agent pays $0 out of
   pocket. CAUTION: this is extending credit; cap exposure (e.g., max 1–2 open
   pay-at-closing invoices at a time, listed properties only, get it in writing)
   until cash flow can absorb slow closings.

## HOAs & property managers (Month 2+)
- HOA boards: group-discount pitch (10+ neighbors same weekend = route density gold)
- Property managers: move-out cleans, common areas — recurring contracts, but slower
  sales cycles and often commercial insurance requirements. Queue behind residential.
