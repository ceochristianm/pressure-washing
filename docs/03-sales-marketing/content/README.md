# Organic Social Content — Light-Touch / Long-Term

> Explicitly NOT a launch priority. Paid + guerrilla + B2B generate jobs faster.
> This exists because footage is being captured anyway and compounding assets are free.

## The system (minimum viable)
- Every job produces raw clips (already required by first-jobs-playbook.md capture checklist)
- Batch-post opportunistically: transformation clips → IG Reels / TikTok / FB page,
  1–3x/week MAX, zero pressure
- Formats that work in this niche: surface-cleaner contrast lines, roof before/after
  timelapse, "what's actually on your driveway" education, day-in-the-life launch story
- The launch story angle ("starting a pressure washing business from zero, documenting
  everything") can build audience — but only worth investing in AFTER the business runs

## Rule
If content ever competes with lead follow-up or job execution time, content loses.
