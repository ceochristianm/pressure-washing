# Meta Ads Playbook

> GATE: do not launch until 2–3 jobs done + speed-to-lead V1 live + GBP has 2+ reviews.

## Campaign structure (launch)
- Objective: Leads → Instant Forms (NOT website traffic; no site needed yet)
- One campaign, 2–3 ad sets by geo (concentrated shortlist areas from zip-code-targeting.md)
- Audience: homeowners 35–65, radius-targeted; keep targeting broad-ish and let
  creative do the qualifying (2026 Meta delivery favors broad + strong creative)
- Budget: start $25–35/day. Ceiling: total ad spend must fit Amex payback math —
  check budget.md before every scale-up.

## Creative (from own job footage — capture per first-jobs-playbook.md)
Priority order:
1. Surface cleaner driveway transformation video (satisfying contrast line, 15–30s)
2. Before/after split-image of a real local job
3. "HOA letter" angle creative (urgency)

Hooks to test:
- "Got an HOA violation letter? Fixed by this weekend."
- "Your driveway isn't dirty. It's growing mold." (education angle)
- "[Area] homeowners: this is what's living on your roof."

## Form + follow-up
- Instant Form fields: Name, Phone, Address ("for your free instant satellite quote")
- Form submit → speed-to-lead automation fires (instant SMS + notify) → human call
  in 1–5 min → 3-tier phone pitch
- Expected benchmarks to calibrate against (unverified, from brainstorm): CPL $18–35,
  close 20–35% of raw leads. Log actuals in data/leads.csv; OUR numbers replace these.

## Scaling rule
Only scale a winning ad set +20–30% at a time, only when: cost per BOOKED JOB < $100
AND schedule has capacity AND Amex clock is safe.
