# Target Market

## Geography
Palm Beach County, FL. Working focus: West Palm Beach, Wellington, Royal Palm Beach,
Palm Beach Gardens, Jupiter, Boca Raton. Detail: `zip-code-targeting.md`.

## Primary: residential homeowners (launch focus)
- Homes 5–15 years old (prime algae/mold age), HOA-governed communities especially —
  HOA violation letters are a built-in urgency trigger ("HOA compliance" angle)
- FL climate = algae regrows annually → every customer is a repeat customer
- Residential washing is FL sales-tax EXEMPT (simple bookkeeping)

## Secondary: real estate agents (B2B, from week 2)
- Need: fast curb-appeal fixes pre-listing/pre-open-house
- Killer offer: pay-at-closing invoicing (invoice on settlement statement, $0 out of
  pocket for the agent) — extreme stickiness
- One productive agent = recurring monthly job flow

## Later: commercial (Month 3+, deliberately deferred)
- Strip malls, restaurants, gas stations — slip-and-fall liability pitch
- Requires: continuous insurance w/ CCC + additional-insured COIs, sales tax handling
- Deferred because: residential is faster to close, tax-simple, and rig-appropriate
