# Competitive Positioning

## The market shape
South FL pressure washing is hyper-competitive at the bottom ($99 driveway "chuck in
a truck" operators) and thin at the top (professional, insured, fast-responding,
premium-experience operators). We compete ONLY at the top.

## Positioning pillars
1. **Speed** — answer in minutes, quote same-call from satellite, book on the spot,
   24-hr availability pitch for realtors. Most competitors take days to reply.
2. **Professionalism as theater** — clean polo, masking-tape ritual on outlets/cameras
   in view of the homeowner, COI offered proactively. Justifies premium price visually.
3. **Safety/technical authority** — "soft-wash, not blast-wash": we explain WHY high
   pressure destroys their siding. Education = trust = price tolerance.
4. **Insured + guaranteed** — $1M policy mentioned in every pitch; plants protected
   by flooding protocol; it's an asset-protection service, not dirt removal.

## What we never do
- Compete on price / match the $99 guys
- Quote a single number by text (3-tier menu, delivered by phone, always)
- Take jobs outside the concentrated target areas at launch (drive time = margin leak)
