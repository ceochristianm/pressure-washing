# Hiring Playbook — Driver / Lead Washer (target: Month 2)

## Hiring gate (don't hire on hope)
ALL true before posting: 3+ weeks of consistent job flow · schedule turning jobs
away or Christian at capacity · cash covers 4+ weeks of wages without new revenue ·
video SOP library ≥ 80% complete.

## The role
Driver + lead washer combined (dad has no license — the hire MUST drive).
- Hard requirements (state in the post): valid FL driver's license, clean record,
  background check + DMV pull consent, physically able (FL heat, hoses, ladders)
- Comp: $18–22/hr base + $15/completed-job production bonus
- Before they drive: added to commercial auto policy as authorized driver — no exceptions

## Sourcing
Indeed + FB jobs + local trade FB groups. Angle: "growing local company, systemized
training (video library), production bonuses, weekly pay."

## Screening
1. Phone screen (5 min): license/record, physical work history, why leaving last job
2. Paid working interview (half day, 1–2 real jobs): watch for care with equipment,
   customer interaction, heat tolerance
3. Background + DMV before offer

## Team structure once hired
| Person | Role |
|---|---|
| Christian | Owner: sales, marketing, ads, remote ops |
| Hire | Driver + lead washer (the physical work) |
| Dad (weekends/as available) | Truck manager/safety lead: chemical mixing, landscaping protection, QC, customer relationship |
| VA | Phones, scheduling, data entry (see va-playbook.md) |
