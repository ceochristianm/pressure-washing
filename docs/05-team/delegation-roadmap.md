# Delegation Roadmap — The Step-Off-The-Truck Sequence

> Target: Medellín ~September. Gate-based, not date-based — each step requires the
> previous one proven.

## Sequence
1. **Now (solo):** Christian does everything; every procedure gets filmed (video SOPs)
2. **VA absorbs the phone** (Month 2): lead calls, booking, data entry. Christian
   still washes. Test: 1 week of VA handling all inbound without booking errors.
3. **Hire absorbs the labor** (Month 2, after hiring gate passes): working interview →
   paired jobs (Christian + hire) for 1–2 weeks → hire + dad-on-weekends solo runs
4. **Christian off the truck** (late Aug target): only doing QC spot-checks, sales
   escalations, ads, and weekly finance review
5. **Remote test** (before flight): 2 consecutive weeks of zero physical intervention.
   If it breaks, fix the system that broke, restart the 2-week clock.
6. **Medellín:** ~2 hrs/day — Monday route review, Wednesday ad metrics, Friday
   payroll/finance review + Claude weekly analysis

## What must be true for step 5 to hold
- Speed-to-lead automation + VA covering 100% of inbound
- Hire trained via video library + production-pay aligned incentives
- Dad or hire holding a payment-collection routine (or 100% digital payment)
- KPI dashboard giving remote visibility (who/what/when per job)
- Commercial auto insurance covering the driver
