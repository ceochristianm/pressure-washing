# VA Playbook (target: Month 2)

## Role scope
Inbound call answering + lead callbacks (scripts provided) · scheduling in CRM ·
lead/job data entry (data/*.csv discipline) · downtime bookkeeping prep ·
later: realtor Instagram DM outreach.

## Sourcing & comp
OnlineJobs.ph, full-time, ~$800–1,200/mo depending on English/sales quality.
Sales-capable VA (can close a 3-tier pitch) is worth the premium — they become the
speed-to-lead human layer when Christian steps back.

## Hard boundaries
- NO direct access to move money from US bank accounts, ever
- Bookkeeping = preparation only: expense categorization matching, weekly commission
  spreadsheet for techs. Christian reviews Friday (10 min) and executes payouts
  himself (Gusto or bank)
- Password manager with role-scoped access, not shared master logins

## Onboarding assets (build during Month 1 solo phase)
- Call scripts (sales-scripts.md) + objection handling
- CRM walkthrough recording
- Data-entry SOP (data/README.md is the spec)
- Escalation rules: what to book solo vs. what pings Christian
