No Protection for Your Gear:
On-demand insurance only covers third-party damage while you are actively on the clock. It does not cover theft. If someone steals your $1,200 commercial pressure washer out of the back of your truck overnight, on-demand insurance will not pay a dime to replace it. Equipment coverage typically requires a continuous monthly policy.
Commercial Work Will Reject It:
If you try to bid on commercial properties (like gas stations, parking lots, or storefronts), property managers usually require you to list them as an "Additional Insured" on a continuous policy. They generally do not accept temporary, hourly certificates.
